Log #6: Use an Ultrasonic Sensor to display distance from the object on the LCD Module.

Resolved
📅 Date: June 4, 2026
Goal

Display the distance of object from the Ultrasonic Sensor in the LCD module.

Obstacle

The display was stuck showing 0999. This was a software timeout indicating MSP430 triggered the sensor but never received the Echo pulse back.

Resolution

Instead of Timer Capture mode, used standard GPIO Port 1 Edge-Triggered Interrupts (PORT1_VECTOR).

Log #5: Display Potentiometer Reading on the LCD Module.

Resolved
📅 Date: June 4, 2026
Goal

Connect Potentiometer to MSP430FR4133 and display the sensor readings on the LCD module.

Obstacle

5th and 6th positions on the LCD fluctuated wildly, while the 2nd and 3rd positions stayed permanently stuck displaying "10xx".

Resolution

potentiometer wire connected to P1.3, but setup in initADC() was configured P1.1 as the input. Enabled the analog function to select bits for P1.3 (P1SEL0 and P1SEL1). Routed the ADC internal multiplexer to Sample Channel A3 (ADCINCH_3).

Log #4: Display Potentiometer Reading on the LCD Module.

Resolved
📅 Date: June 4, 2026
Goal

Connect Potentiometer to MSP430FR4133 and display the sensor readings on the LCD module.

Obstacle

In the original showNumber function, raw arbitrary integers (2, 4, 6) were being passed directly into showChar().

Resolution

Rewrote digit extraction logic in showNumber to scale up to 4 digits, and explicitly linked them to pins using hardware positions lcdPositions[2] to lcdPositions[5].
